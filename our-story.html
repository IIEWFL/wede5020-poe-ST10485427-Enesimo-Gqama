Our Humble Beginning

"Botshabelo" is a Tswana word meaning "place to run to". Botshabelo is a place of refuge and development which was established as an NPO in Midrand near Johannesburg in 2000. it began as a safe heaven for abandoned, orphaned and vulnerable new-borns and young children, offering comprehensive therapy in a nurturing family setting. Recognising the greater need in the community, we expaned its purpose include a preschool, which provides outstanding early childhood development to both residents and children from surrounding underprivileged community.

Our Mission

To make a difference in the lives of the children who need it the most, through education or residential care, we seek to provide children with the best possible start in life.

Our Vision

To break the cycle of neglect by nurturing resilient, educated and hopeful future leaders.
